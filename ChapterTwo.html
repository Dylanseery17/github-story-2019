Shifting Seasons


Chapter Two

The awareness still spread world wide as protests and summits commenced throughout the month of March. People began to take action by marching the streets of every city in the world demanding solidarity and eminent change. World leaders began to cooperate to make a bigger impact on the globe by reducing the use of fossil fuels and deforestation. Funds and grants where setup to encourage people to switch to electric vehicles, cycle or use public transport.

The real issue was not the everyday individual it was the Multi national companies and factories across the world using fossil fuels. Taxes and tariffs where set on fossil fuels hoping that we would see an increase in renewable energy. Smart car companies like tesla and byton received government funding for charging points and to make there vehicles more affordable. World class scientists and engineers where set the tasks to research the topic and try to find solutions.Some key facts found by scientists where shocking. Several hundred million fewer people will be exposed to climate-related risks and susceptible to poverty by 2050. Net emissions of carbon dioxide would need to fall by about 45% from 2010 levels by 2030, reaching "net zero" around 2050. Are We capable of this change?
