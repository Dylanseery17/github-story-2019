Chapter Three

People began to take notice to all the talk of global warming and began to take public transport, car-pool and cycle more often a drastic change was made by humanity. Not everyone participated but the ones who did had a snow ball effect on there friends and family's. Social media challenges where set to donate to charities who would help against global warming. Schools began to teach the importance of recycling and global warming to the younger generations.

Multinational Companies and organisations found better methods to use there energy and resources due to taxes and tariffs. They where offered incentives to encourage there workers to cycle to work so bike to work schemes where put in place to benefit everyone.

Research continued on the topic but we began to see a more stable world.The throughout Spring would see a record of the least amount of wildfires compared the last 10 previous years. Co2 emissions also hit a low throughout the month of May. The impact was visible in such a short amount of time with all the bad things happening throughout the world it was nice and rewarding to people to see something positive they had an impact on.
