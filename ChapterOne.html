Shifting Seasons


Chapter One

The year is 2025 and the time has come. The awareness for global warming has spread across the globe. Climate Change has effected the season of spring as flowers start to blossom and the birds began to chirp weeks in advance. The blazing wildfires have become more frequent killing people by the thousands. Hurricanes are becoming more fierce and viscous and the sea level is rising at a rapid rate. The recognition of global warming has increased as it spreads throughout the world by either protests, social media or meetings involving the worlds leaders.

We are not only effecting humanity but we are endangering wildlife throughout the planet. Habitat destruction due to climate change and deforestation has already eradicated many animals. The Amazon rain forest, Arctic region and coral reefs of the world are merely what they where just 5 years ago.

If action is not taking we wont have a planet in year's to come. This is the first issue to arise where the co-operation of humanity is vital for survival. As much as all the destruction hate politics and religion that separate us we must find ourselves to bond together to tackle this situation. It wont be easy it will take great leadership and co-operation. We wont reverse what damage we have already done but at least we can make a change for the better and for the future of our planet. The real question is if we will...
